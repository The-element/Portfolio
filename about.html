About Me

I love challenges and I try to avoid beign bored. I'm not one of those who stands down when a challenge occur.
If I can't buy it, I create it. And, most of the time the economy has its limits so I read, I learn and I get my hands dirty. And, While we're at it why not do it to the full extent.
I've created webpages, saunas, remodelled and refurbished cars, I've created furniture and restored broken tablets at game consoles.
I live in Stockholm with my lovely girlfriend from 13 years back. We are both from up north, from a town by the name of Skellefteå, but moved here 2010.
I thrive here, don´t get me wrong, I love my hometown but it's in Stockholm the opportunities are present.
I love science, technology and progress, and I´d like to make a difference somehow, someday.
Maybe that´s why my dream is to work for a company like Google, Gates foundation, Space X or something similar, with the potential of changing the world.
Who knows, maybe I´ll study machine learning and computer science in the future.

I also like to interact with people in my surroundings. I get to form valuable relationships, to test my abilities verbally and logically, and I get to participate in interesting discussions.
Maybe that's why I fit in so well where I'm currently at, at ESSVE Sverige, where I work mainly as a customer service representative.
I´ve been working towards sales but I started feeling kind'a stuck so for the past four years I´ve been working in a lot of areas. To challenge myself.
I started a small business on the side as a way to work with one of my many hobbies, the company was named Nordr, here I sold furniture and interior that's designed and developed by myself.
I learned alot about running a small company. For example the importance of contracting, bookkeeping, design, communication, deliveries, etc. I ran this for almost 3 years without actually doing any large investments, before coming to the point I had to go all in, or try something else.
By that time I had found myself a new passion; programming. I had created the website for my company, on the wordpress- and woocommerce-platform and I had started with alot of home automation.
Today my whole house is connected. I have routines and voice activation of lights, heating, cleaning, electricity, home media center, etc.
During this time, I also managed social media for ESSVE during a time period at the same time as I´m trying to form a new process for managing complaints.
And, I've also worked with Buildflow/Byggvarulistan on the side, with their database, to help them get sorted with their price comparacing tool.
The past year thou, I decided I needed to put my full focus on what I really wanted to do, so I started studying with an intense bootcamp in Irland, to become a full stack software developer. A choice I haven't regreted since.
