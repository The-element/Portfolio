About Me

I love a challenge and i hate having nothing to do. I'm not one of those who stands down when a challenge occur.
I hold my breath, take a step backwards and then strike with full power. If you need to do it, why not do it to the full extent.
In that case you have assured that you won´t need to do it twice.
I live in Stockholm, with my girlfriend and my dog (a Chihuahua). We moved here 2010 but I´m actually from up north, from a town by the name of Skellefteå.
I thrive here, don´t get me wrong, I love my hometown but it´s in Stockholm the opportunities are present.
I love science, technology and progress, and I´d like to make a difference somehow, someday.
Maybe that´s why my dream is to work for a company like Google or Space X.
Who knows, maybe I´ll study machine learning and computer science in the future.
I also like to interact with people in my surroundings. I get to form valuable relationships, to test my abilities verbally and logically, and I get to participate in interesting discussions.
I´ve been working towards sales these past few years but recently I´ve been working in a lot of areas. I´ve started a small side business, for fun.
I´m currently managing social media at the same time as I´m trying to form a new process for managing complaints, at ESSVE.
And, I´m working with Buildflow/Byggvarulistan on the side to help them get sorted with their price tool.
